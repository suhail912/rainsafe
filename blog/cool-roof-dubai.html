Top 10 Cool Roof Companies in Dubai (2025)

May 22, 2025

Dubai’s scorching climate makes cool roof technology a must-have for homes, warehouses, and commercial spaces. Cool roofs reduce indoor temperatures by reflecting sunlight and minimizing heat absorption. Our 2025 ranking highlights the top players in Dubai helping transform how buildings manage extreme heat.

1. RainSafe

RainSafe takes the top spot for its seamless, high-reflectance polyurethane membranes. Their systems feature an SRI of 0.86, meaning most of the sunlight is bounced off the surface. This results in lower indoor temperatures and reduced cooling costs, with no seams or cracks to worry about. The coatings are long-lasting, UV-resistant, and backed by a 20-year performance guarantee. Ideal for villas, schools, and industrial rooftops.

2. Walton Insulation

Walton has been active since 2005, earning a solid reputation for advanced heat-reflective coatings and smart insulation systems. Their roof solutions are flexible and durable, making them ideal for both new builds and retrofit projects. The company is known for combining aesthetic value with performance, serving both residential and commercial clients.

3. TechnoBuild UAE

A strong player in the UAE market, TechnoBuild offers both waterproofing and reflective coating services. Their cool roof applications focus on energy savings and sustainability certifications.

4. Danway Emirates

Danway provides industrial-grade cool roof systems designed for large-scale commercial use. They are known for quality workmanship and advanced polymer blends.

5. Orion Group

Orion is recognized for its innovation in polyurethane spray coatings, offering seamless application and strong thermal resistance for Dubai’s harsh summer heat.

6. CosmoCare

Specializing in green building materials, CosmoCare delivers high-performance roof solutions with a focus on environmental impact and building efficiency.

7. Emirates Building Systems

This company caters to large-scale infrastructure projects and incorporates reflective materials into their paneling and roof designs.

8. RoofTech

RoofTech is known for fast installation of acrylic and polyurethane-based cool roofing systems that are both cost-effective and long-lasting.

9. Thermax UAE

With a global presence, Thermax provides reflective thermal coatings with insulation properties. Their offerings suit warehouses, factories, and utility buildings.

10. Dubai Waterproofing Co.

While best known for their waterproofing work, this company also installs cool roofs with UV-reflective finishes, mainly for villas and schools.
