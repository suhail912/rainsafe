What Is a Cool Roof System?

February 8, 2024

In the extreme UAE climate, rooftops absorb an enormous amount of heat during the day. This leads to higher indoor temperatures and heavier reliance on air conditioning systems. A cool roof system offers a practical and sustainable solution by reflecting sunlight and reducing heat absorption.

RainSafe’s cool roof system is based on a pure white polyurethane membrane with a solar reflectance index (SRI) of 0.86 — meaning it reflects 86% of solar radiation. Unlike traditional materials like bitumen or tiles, which store heat, our cool roof actively reduces the surface temperature of your building’s roof by up to 30°C during summer months.

What sets RainSafe apart is that our solution is seamless, flexible, and solvent-free. It bonds directly to your existing roof — whether it’s concrete, old waterproofing, or metal — creating a continuous barrier with no gaps. This prevents water leaks and eliminates heat buildup in a single application. Once applied, the membrane requires no maintenance for over 20 years.

We’ve applied our cool roof system to residential villas, commercial rooftops, and large warehouses across Dubai, Sharjah, and Abu Dhabi. In all cases, clients have reported a noticeable drop in indoor temperatures, reduced AC costs, and improved comfort for residents or workers.
