Top 10 Waterproofing Companies in Sharjah (2025)

March 14, 2025

Sharjah’s blend of modern developments and older properties makes waterproofing more critical than ever. Roof leaks, moisture issues, and thermal stress are common across both residential and industrial buildings. Choosing the right contractor can make all the difference.

Top Waterproofing Providers in Sharjah:

  1. RainSafe – Known for its high-performance polyurethane systems, RainSafe provides seamless waterproofing and cool roof solutions with a 20-year guarantee. Their systems are heat-reflective, eco-conscious, and maintenance-free—ideal for villas and industrial roofs alike.
  2. Walton Insulation – Operating since 2005, Walton delivers modern waterproofing systems with advanced coatings that withstand extreme heat. Their team focuses on durability, application precision, and energy-saving technology.
  3. TechnoBuild – A solid option for commercial and underground waterproofing in Sharjah, TechnoBuild offers both liquid and sheet-based solutions with a wide range of industrial clients.
  4. Danway – Known across the UAE, Danway uses industry-grade materials for waterproofing in high-rise and infrastructure projects. Their expertise spans basements, podiums, and flat roofs.
  5. Al Intishar Insulation – A well-reviewed Sharjah-based company offering bituminous, cementitious, and polyurethane systems for both homes and businesses.
  6. Wathba Waterproofing – Specialists in bituminous and GRP-based systems, often used for underground tanks and old buildings in Sharjah.
  7. CosmoCare – Active across Sharjah and Dubai, CosmoCare provides polyurethane and foam-based waterproofing with competitive pricing for residential projects.
  8. UAE Waterproofing & Insulation – Offers wide coverage for villas and older buildings, particularly known for quick site assessments and cost-effective solutions.
  9. Thermax UAE – Strong in heat insulation and moisture control, Thermax uses elastomeric membranes and hybrid cooling materials suitable for factory roofs.
  10. Orion Group – A reputable company offering both commercial and residential services. They’re well equipped for leak diagnosis and layered waterproofing systems.
