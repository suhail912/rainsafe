The Power of Pure Polymer Polyurethane from Greece

Published: May 2025

What Makes Our Waterproofing Unique?

At RainSafe, we use only pure polymer polyurethane sourced from Greece—a product awarded the European ETA 21/0249 certification for safety, sustainability, and performance. Unlike cheap blends, our system is made for extreme climates like the UAE.

Benefits of Pure Polymer Polyurethane

  • Exceptional waterproofing and durability
  • Environmentally friendly and non-toxic
  • Long-lasting protection—over 20 years with no maintenance
  • Superior flexibility and crack-bridging ability

Why Trust European Technology?

European standards for waterproofing materials are the strictest in the world. Our polyurethane system is certified for both quality and environmental responsibility, making it the top choice for sustainable construction in the UAE.
