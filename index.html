About RainSafe

RainSafe by Walton Insulation L.L.C. specializes in sustainable waterproofing and heat insulation for houses, villas, and residential buildings across the UAE. Our solutions combine leak protection and cooling benefits using high-quality, eco-friendly materials with a lifespan of over 20 years.

  • Over 20 years of experience and 2,000+ completed house and villa projects
  • Solutions awarded the European ETA 21/0249 assessment
  • Maintenance-free, energy-efficient & sustainable
  • Protect roofs from rain, moisture, rust, and root growth
  • Reduce air conditioning costs by lowering roof temperatures
